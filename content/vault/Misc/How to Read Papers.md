### Order to Read
- Title
- Abstract
- Summary/Conclusions
	- Now at this point if they don't feel relevant to you then don't read that paper.
- Tables and Figures
	- They can be very info dense but they deliver a clear sense of what the authors wanted to do.
- If its all good till now then
- Read the intro/theory
- Read Results
- Read Methods to understand their experiments, assumptions , etc
